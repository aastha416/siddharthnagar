KALA NAMAK RICE

            kala namak rice is has been cultivated since
            the Buddhist period(600 BC)
    
            Kala namak grains were found from the
            excavation of kapilvastu.
    
            kala namak rice is rich in iron and zinc
            has low glycemic index.
    
            kala namak rice is grown in the TARAI area 
            of siddharth nagar
